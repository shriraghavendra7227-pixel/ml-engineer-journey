for Loop

-   Repeats over a sequence. Syntax: for i in range(5): print(i)

range(start, stop, step)

while Loop

-   Runs while a condition is True.

break

-   Exits the nearest loop immediately.

continue

-   Skips the current iteration.

Nested Loops

-   Loop inside another loop.
